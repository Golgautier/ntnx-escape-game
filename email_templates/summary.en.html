Nutanix Cloud Infrastructure (NCI) & Prism

Prism

Prism Central is the main interface when interacting with a Nutanix platform. It groups together almost all the services hosted on our cloud platform, and governs all or part of the Nutanix clusters you own.

Nutanix has done its utmost to make the Prism Central experience simple and intuitive, because we believe that you don't need to be an expert to manage an IT platform on a day-to-day basis.

Multitenancy/RBAC

You have experienced this through the use of projects and the assignment of roles. The Nutanix platform enables you to natively manage a multi-user approach, interconnecting with your directories and associating predefined or customised roles with very fine granularity. Each connected user only sees the assets to which they have rights, just as they do for services, thus avoiding any unauthorised interaction or indiscretion with other users.

The power of categories

Nutanix provides a notion of category, in key/value format, which can be associated with the majority of cluster elements (clusters, nodes, VMs, images, networks, etc.). These categories enable :

  • Quick identification of elements via searches using these categories
  • User rights management for these categorised elements
  • Association with management policies (protection, security, storage or affinity)

These categories can also be used by your backup tools to identify VMs to be protected.

Network management

Integrated IPAM

With AHV, Nutanix provides an integrated IPAM. When you create your subnet, you can define a range of IPs that will be allocated to VMs configured using DHCP and deployed in this network. Practical.

Software Defined Network

Also with AHV, Nutanix offers a Software Defined Network solution. Among other things, this solution enables you to:

  • Define micro-segmentation rules: filter network exchanges between your VMs, and between your VMs and the outside world.
  • Define private networks isolating your VMs from the rest of the cluster, and enabling the same IP address to be reused, for testing purposes for example.

Integrated Disaster Recovery

Production needs to be protected, and backup is often not the best solution for quick recovery. To achieve this, Nutanix provides the ability to drive snapshots automatically (without storage costs or impact on performance) and replicate them to a second site if you wish, with an RPO starting at 0. Restarts can be orchestrated automatically (via witness) or manually, so that you can orchestrate the restart yourself to make services available in the order that best suits the operation.

Distributed, resilient and secure storage

Nutanix is the pioneer of hyperconvergence, the principle of which is to pool the compute and storage resources of traditional servers into a single pool of resources to be consumed by VMs. In terms of storage, the hyperconvergence solution must manage data resilience and security. Nutanix has made technical choices to optimise the performance of distributed storage, to reduce reconstruction times in the event of disk/node failures and to natively and efficiently manage all data-reduction options (compression, deduplication and/or erasure-coding) as well as encryption (software or hardware).

Scaling

The Nutanix solution was developed with the idea of providing the same flexibility as the public cloud, and in particular with its ability to grow without constraints. The main idea is to avoid buying too much and overestimating your needs over the next 5 years. With Nutanix, you choose a solution that allows you to host your short-term needs, and then increase capacity later by adding new nodes. Nutanix manages the addition of nodes very simply (as you have seen), while allowing you to have heterogeneous hardware, and ensuring a stable level of performance despite the increasing power of your workloads.

Easy and safe upgrades

With LCM, Nutanix makes it possible to update its software and hardware stack in just a few clicks. Forget the hours spent comparing compatibility matrices and defining upgrade paths for your IS: Nutanix now does it for you. In LCM, tick the products that are proposed for upgrade, and the platform will check the prerequisites and define the order of upgrades. Come back a few hours later and your platform will be up to date, without any service interruption.

Nutanix Cloud Manager (NCM)

In addition to the core NCI component, Nutanix offers a range of functions designed to help customers maintain their information systems in operational condition. Here is an overview.

Customized Dashboards and reports

The Prism central interface can be customised to create dashboards tailored to the needs of each user. These dashboards can then be shared between users for greater efficiency. Prism also allows the definition of fully personalised reports, which can be generated on demand or automatically, with the potential to be sent to the recipients you see fit.

Sizing recommendations

Nutanix monitors your VMs on a daily basis, and will provide you with recommendations on how to size them. Too much CPU, not enough RAM, a VM that doesn't do anything, don't look for it yourself, all this information will come back to you in the form of alerts. Your immediate benefits: resource savings and better SLAs for your applications.

Smart capacity planning

Nutanix helps its customers by providing an intelligent capacity-planning engine, capable of estimating when your cluster will be loaded, and giving you 1 year's notice, leaving you time to organise your budget. Are you planning an acquisition? A new application to deploy? Enter your new requirements, and capacity planning will make recommendations for the most appropriate additions (type and number of nodes, date of addition).

Low-code automation

Everyone needs automation, but not everyone is a developer. To remedy this, Nutanix provides a low-code automation solution that lets you define a trigger followed by actions of your choice. Simple and effective. For which use-cases?

  • Automatic categorisation of VMs
  • Automatic CMDB update
  • Auto-remediation of incorrectly-sorted VMs

Automation with marketplace

Nutanix provides its 'Self-Service' solution for customers looking for more advanced automation. This multi-cloud automation engine enables blueprints to be defined and deployed via a marketplace, simplifying access to this service catalog for business teams. Deploy your applications in the same way in the cloud or on-prem and implement approval workflows to control deployments.

Security dashboard

Nutanix detects asset vulnerabilities and compliance with current regulations, on a single dashboard. It doesn't just concern your data-centre assets, you can also integrate your public cloud assets into the same tool, so you have a global view of the security of your IS.

What you didn't see

NKP : Nutanix Kubernetes Platform

Nutanix offers a solution for deploying Kubernetes upstreams clusters at scale and across multiple clouds. Its features include:

  • Easy deployment, updating and scaling of Kubernetes clusters.
  • Deployment of pre-configured and pre-validated application stacks to ensure the interoperability of all these elements.
  • Solutions to check the proper operation of clusters, and the application of best practices to them and the applications.

NC2 : Nutanix Cloud Cluster

Do you need to move into the cloud but are worried about the costs and risks of lock-in? Nutanix lets you deploy Nutanix clusters with Cloud operators, and manage these assets in the same way as you do on your on-prem infrastructure, in the same interface.

  • No additional knowledge required.
  • Migration of your DCs to the Cloud is simplified and accelerated (simple VM 'migration').
  • No lock-in, because you can change cloud-provider by migrating your assets from one NC2 cluster to another.

NUS : Nutanix Unified Storage

Nutanix offers several integrated storage solutions on its cloud platform:

  • Block storage (iSCSI)
  • File storage (NFS and SMB/CIFS)
  • Object storage (S3)

All these services can be deployed on the platform in just a few clicks, enabling you to take advantage of them quickly and easily, with enterprise-grade features. You can also take advantage of Data Lens, which lets you map your data, report suspicious users, suspected ransomware, etc...

Nutanix Cost Governance

With NCM, Nutanix also provides a tool for managing your costs: associate your on-prem and cloud assets with cost centres/business units, monitor cost trends for each of them, check compliance with a budget, and apply the savings recommendations made by the solution. A solution that will enable you to visualise and control your on prem and cloud spending.
